Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer widely regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world. He was born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India.
Kohli made his international debut in August 2008, representing India in One Day Internationals (ODIs) against Sri Lanka. He later made his Test debut in June 2011 against the West Indies.
Kohli has been the captain of the Indian cricket team in all formats (Test, ODI, and T20I). He took over the captaincy in Test matches in 2014 and became the limited-overs captain in 2017.
Kohli has achieved numerous records in his cricketing career. As of my knowledge cutoff in September 2021, he holds the record for the fastest player to score 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 runs in ODI cricket.
